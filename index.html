Actively Designing the Future

Hi, I'm Joe. I'm a senior at Virginia Tech studying Computer Science and Industrial Design, who's building a better future through user-centered design.

DEVELOPER

Developer by education, I am proficient in Java and Javascript, with extensive experince in C. I have been building Android applications for over two years and have experience working on projects ranging in size from small single page apps to full sized enterprise applications. My apps aim to solve common problems with simple intuitive design, from finding a place to nap on campus to choosing your mode of transportation.

DESIGNER

A passion for design drives me to create usable, beautiful products. At Virginia Tech I am an Industrial Design minor and am heavily involved in Design for America and the Institute for Creativity, Arts, and Technology. I believe design can be used to create the next generation of consumer software that is not only visually beautiful, but also usable, efficient, and accessible.
